About SmartEdu

SmartEdu is a comprehensive student portal designed to help students track their academic progress, monitor attendance, and access educational resources. The platform provides real-time data and analytics to help students make informed decisions about their education.

Our features include attendance tracking, GPA monitoring, performance analytics, AI-powered assistance, and more. The platform is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to all students.
